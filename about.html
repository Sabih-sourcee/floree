About Floree

A small routine, chosen with care.

Why we keep the shelf small, and what we're building next.

Who we are

Skincare only earns a spot here if it works

Floree is a Pakistan-based skincare edit built around one idea: a routine only works if you'll actually stick to it. That's why we keep the shelf small — one cleanser, one treatment, one SPF — each one chosen for how it performs, not how it's packaged.

Right now, our shelf is a curated edit of trusted, tried-and-tested products we source and vet ourselves. Alongside that, we're quietly developing our own in-house Floree formulas — the first of which are coming soon.

Every order is confirmed and shipped through a real conversation, not a checkout form. Message us on WhatsApp or Instagram and we'll walk you through what's right for your skin.

Why Floree exists

Born from 12-step fatigue

We kept watching people buy drawers full of serums, then quit halfway — not because skincare doesn't work, but because the routine was too loud to live with. Floree started as a quieter alternative: three steps you'd still be doing a month later.

That insight still shapes everything. We don't pad the shelf. We don't invent a new “must-have” every week. We curate what earns its place, ship it nationwide through a real WhatsApp chat, and we're building our own formulas for the same simple philosophy.

If you've ever felt overwhelmed by skincare advice — you're exactly who this edit is for.

What we stand for

Three things we won't compromise on

Curated

Every product is picked and tested before it's ever offered — not added just to pad the shelf.

Honest

We're upfront about what's ours and what we source — no pretending a resold product is something it isn't.

Simple

Three products, not thirty. A routine you'll actually finish, not one that sits half-used in a drawer.

Simple by design, honest by default.
