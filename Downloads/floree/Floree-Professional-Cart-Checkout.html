Meet the Founder

Built with a clear purpose.

Saif Ur Rehman — Founder, Floree

Floree is a young skincare venture founded by Saif Ur Rehman. He started the brand recently with a straightforward goal: introduce quality-focused skincare products to the market without making everyday routines complicated or overwhelming.

The journey began with a carefully selected range of practical essentials for cleansing, hydration and sun protection. Instead of building a crowded catalogue, Saif focused on products that answer real daily skincare needs, clear product presentation, useful bundle pricing and responsive customer support.

As Floree grows, the vision is to keep raising the standard of the products offered, earn long-term customer trust and gradually introduce more quality skincare — including Floree's own product line — while preserving the personal service the brand started with.

Simple routines. Quality-conscious products. Honest service from the first message to delivery.
